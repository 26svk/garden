Our Species

Species in the Garden

The Vend Utca 12 Garden is very biodiverse, containing many types of plants, animals, and insects!

Plants

There are a variety of Plants in the Garden, ranging from Bamboo to the European Plum.
Flowers, ferns, trees, bushes, shrubs, and fruits are the main features of the garden, making it a great place to be!

Animals

There are a variety of Animals in the Garden, ranging from Domestic Cats to the Red Squirrel.
A family of a few cats, all of different patterns and colors, gather in our garden to have a meal or play around.
Squirrels, birds, and lizards make themselves home too!

Insects

There are a variety of Insects in the Garden, ranging from the Asian Lady Beetle to Southern Oak Bush-Cricket.
Insects can be found in every direction here. Some are too small to get easily noticed, while others,
such as the Bumblebee buzz around all day!

Our Facilities
